Who is Isaac Newton?

Sir Isaac Newton was an English physicist and mathematician who revolutionized the understanding of motion with his formulation of three fundamental laws. These laws, published in his seminal work "Philosophiæ Naturalis Principia Mathematica" (Mathematical Principles of Natural Philosophy) in 1687, form the basis of classical mechanics and were the dominant scientific viewpoint for centuries.

Did You Know?

Newton's laws of motion are fundamental to understanding and navigating space today. his three laws of motion provide the foundation for understanding how objects move in space, including the orbits of planets, satellites, and spacecraft.
